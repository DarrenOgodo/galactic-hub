Saturn Says...

Repeat the flashing pattern to win. The number of flashing colours increases each round.

The further you progress, the faster the colours flash and the less time you have to repeat the pattern.

The score on the left is your high score and the score on the right is your current score. High scores are device specific.

Colours will flash 3 times synchronously if you fail in repeating the pattern. Good luck!
